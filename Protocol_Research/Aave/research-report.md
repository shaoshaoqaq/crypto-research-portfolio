# Aave Protocol Research


## Research Question

Why did Aave become one of the largest DeFi lending protocols?


## Executive Summary

Aave solves the problem of on-chain capital efficiency by creating a permissionless lending market.

Users can supply assets to earn yield and borrow assets through over-collateralized loans.

Its success comes from:

1. Permissionless liquidity
2. Strong risk management
3. Multi-chain deployment
4. DeFi composability

# 7. Market Judgment


## Why Aave Works


Aave succeeds because:


1.

It matches crypto-native users' needs.


2.

It creates a permissionless credit-like market.


3.

It becomes DeFi infrastructure.



## Bear Case


Aave may face limitations because:


- over collateralization restricts users
- competition from Morpho
- lack of real credit


## Final View


Aave is likely to remain core DeFi infrastructure, but future growth depends on whether DeFi can expand beyond crypto-native users.
