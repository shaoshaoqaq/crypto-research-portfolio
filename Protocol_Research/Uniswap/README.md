# Uniswap V3 Protocol Research


## Research Question

Why did Uniswap V3's concentrated liquidity mechanism improve AMM capital efficiency?


## Research Object


Product:

Uniswap V3


Category:

DEX / AMM


Version Focus:

Uniswap V3



---

# Research Summary


Uniswap is a decentralized exchange protocol based on Automated Market Maker (AMM) mechanisms.

Unlike traditional order-book exchanges, Uniswap allows users to trade directly against liquidity pools.

Uniswap V3 introduced concentrated liquidity, allowing liquidity providers to allocate capital within customized price ranges instead of the entire price curve.

This research analyzes:

- Why traditional AMM had capital efficiency problems
- How concentrated liquidity solves this problem
- How Uniswap creates value for traders and LPs
- Future opportunities and risks



---

# Key Findings


## 1. Problem


Traditional AMM models distribute liquidity across the entire price range.

However, most trading activity happens near the current market price.

Therefore:

- Large amounts of liquidity remain unused
- LP capital efficiency is low
- Traders experience higher price impact



---

## 2. Innovation


Uniswap V3 introduces:


### Concentrated Liquidity


LPs can choose specific price ranges.

Capital is concentrated where trading is most likely to occur.


Result:

- Higher liquidity depth
- Lower slippage
- Higher fee efficiency



---

## 3. Market Position


Uniswap succeeded because it created a permissionless liquidity infrastructure.

Its advantages:

- No permission listing
- Strong liquidity network effect
- DeFi composability



---

## 4. Main Risks


However:


- LP management becomes more complex
- Professional market makers may dominate liquidity
- MEV affects trading experience
