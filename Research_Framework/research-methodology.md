# Crypto Research Methodology


## Research Philosophy

My research process focuses on understanding crypto protocols from first principles.

The framework:

Problem

↓

Protocol Design

↓

Mechanism Analysis

↓

User Demand

↓

Data Validation

↓

Competitive Analysis

↓

Risk Assessment

↓

Final Judgment



# Research Process


## 1. Define Research Question

Before researching any protocol:

- What problem does it solve?
- Why does this problem matter?
- What alternative solutions exist?



## 2. Understand Protocol Mechanism

Analyze:

- Core architecture
- Economic incentives
- User behavior
- Technical innovation



## 3. Identify Users

Understand:

- Who uses the protocol?
- Why do users choose it?
- What alternatives exist?



## 4. Validate With Data

Use:

- DeFiLlama
- Dune Analytics
- Blockchain explorers


Analyze:

- TVL
- Volume
- Revenue
- User activity



## 5. Competitive Analysis

Compare:

- Product design
- Market position
- Network effects
- Growth potential



## 6. Risk Analysis

Identify:

- Technical risks
- Economic risks
- Market risks
- Regulatory risks



## 7. Final Judgment

Answer:

Why can this protocol succeed?

Why might it fail?

What should we monitor?
